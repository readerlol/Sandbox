Coca-Cola

The Coca-Cola Company is an American multinational corporation founded in 1892.

Coca-Cola was developed in 1886 by pharmacist John Stith Pemberton.

The product contained cocaine from coca leaves and caffeine from kola nuts which together acted as a stimulant.
